# Letter for Living Poster Brief

## Theme
- English: The Ground Beneath
- Korean: 믿음

## Verse
- Reference: 히브리서 11:1
- English (ESV): Now faith is the assurance of things hoped for, the conviction of things not seen.
- Korean (개역개정): 믿음은 바라는 것들의 실상이요 보이지 않는 것들의 증거니.

## Emphasis
- Primary words: faith, assurance
- Secondary words: hope, conviction
- Rationale: To highlight the essence of faith as the foundation and assurance in life.

## Layout
- Hierarchy: Title > Verse > Theme
- Alignment: Center
- Margins: 20mm
- Spacing rhythm: 10mm between elements

## Typography
- Primary style: Serif for title
- Secondary style: Sans-serif for verse
- Contrast: High contrast between title and verse

## Color
- Palette: Earth tones (browns, greens)
- Background: Light beige
- Rationale: To evoke a sense of grounding and stability.

## Production Notes
- Size: 420×594 mm (재단), 422×596 mm (작업) vertical
- Export: Ensure high-quality print for clarity.
