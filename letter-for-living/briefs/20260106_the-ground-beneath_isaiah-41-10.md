# Letter for Living Poster Brief

## Theme
- English: The Ground Beneath
- Korean: 믿음

## Verse
- Reference: Isaiah 41:10
- English (ESV): Fear not, for I am with you; be not dismayed, for I am your God;
- Korean (개역개정): 두려워하지 말라, 내가 너와 함께 함이니라; 놀라지 말라, 나는 네 하나님이니라;

## [1] 메인 포스터 문장
- EN: I am with you.
- KO: 내가 너와 함께 하노라.

## [2] 서브 문장 / 강조 단어
- Do not be afraid.

## [3] 감정 키워드
- Faith, Strength, Companionship, Reassurance, Courage

## [4] 디자인 방향
- A balanced layout with the main line centered, surrounded by ample whitespace. The tone is quiet yet strong, with subtle emphasis on the keywords. The overall density should evoke a sense of calm and support.

## [5] 추천 공간
- Ideal for a living room or study area, creating a serene atmosphere.

## Production Notes
- Size: 420×594 mm (재단), 422×596 mm (작업) vertical
